ACM-India Annual Event

ACM India announces its annual event for the year 2021 to be held at the Indian Institute of Technology, Gandhinagar, on the 13th of February, 2021.

ACM, the Association for Computing Machinery is the world's largest educational and scientific society, uniting computing educators, researchers and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources and address the field's challenges. ACM awards the Turing Award (aka Noble Prize in Computing).

ACM supports the professional growth of its members by providing opportunities for life-long learning, career development, and professional networking. ACM is recording a healthy growth in India, and ACM India was launched in 2010 to increase the focus on the country.

IRISS 2021 and the ACM-W Workshop on Women in Computer Science and Research are co-located ACM events.

Who can attend?

ACM India has been organizing annual flagship events to discuss trends in science and technology, and to celebrate ACM's spirit and India's accomplishments in computing. This event is attended by ACM Turing Award winners, ACM Office Bearers, students, researchers, and professionals in computer science and allied areas. Registration is free but required. FAQ

Do I have to pay to attend the ACM India Annual Event? No. Registration is mandatory and free. Please register using the links on this page (will be provided soon).
I only want to attend the ACM India Annual Event and the ACM-W workshop. Do I need to register for ARCS? No. Please register for both events using their respective registration forms(will be provided soon) if you only plan to attend these two events (and not ARCS).
I have already registered for ARCS. Should I register again for the annual event? No, as long as you indicated that you would be attending the annual event in the ARCS registration form. If you neglected to do this then please feel free register (will be provided soon) separately for the annual event.
What does the registration for ARCS cover? Registration for ARCS covers attending the entire event virtually and an opportunity to participate in all the competitions. In addition, research scholars who will be presenting their selected abstracts will also receive a complementary one-year ACM membership.
